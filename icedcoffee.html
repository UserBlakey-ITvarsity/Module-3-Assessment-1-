Classic Iced Coffee

Prep Time: 5 mins

Yield: 2 servings

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups brewed coffee, chilled
  • 1/2 cup milk (or plant-based alternative)
  • 2 tablespoons sugar or sweetener
  • Ice cubes
  • Whipped cream (optional)

Steps:

  1. Brew coffee and let it chill.
  2. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
  3. Pour chilled coffee over the ice.
  4. Add milk and sugar, stirring well.
  5. Top with whipped cream if desired.
  6. Enjoy your refreshing iced coffee!
