Malva Pudding

Prep Time: 20 mins

Yield: 6 servings

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon apricot jam

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F) and grease a baking dish.
  2. Mix flour, baking soda, and salt in a bowl.
  3. Cream sugar and butter together, then add the egg and apricot jam.
  4. Combine dry ingredients with wet mixture and stir in milk and vinegar.
  5. Pour batter into the baking dish and bake for about 30 minutes until golden.
  6. Prepare a simple sauce of warmed butter, sugar, milk, and vanilla.
  7. Pour the sauce over the warm pudding and let it soak in.
  8. Serve warm with custard or ice cream and enjoy!
