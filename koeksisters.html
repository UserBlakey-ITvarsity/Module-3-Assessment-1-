Koeksisters

Prep Time: 30 mins

Yield: 10 servings

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • Oil for frying
  • Sugar syrup for coating

Steps:

  1. Mix flour, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Add milk, egg, and melted butter, kneading until smooth.
  3. Roll out and cut dough into strips, twisting into braids.
  4. Deep-fry in hot oil until golden brown.
  5. Dip into chilled sugar syrup while warm.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
