Refreshing Lemonade

Prep Time: 5 mins

Yield: 2 servings

Ingredients:

  • 2 fresh lemons
  • 2 cups cold water
  • 2 tablespoons sugar or honey
  • Ice cubes
  • Fresh mint leaves (optional)

Steps:

  1. Juice the lemons and remove any seeds.
  2. Mix lemon juice with cold water and sugar or honey until dissolved.
  3. Add ice cubes for a refreshing chill.
  4. Garnish with fresh mint leaves if desired.
  5. Serve chilled and enjoy your homemade lemonade!
