Hertzoggies

Prep Time: 25 mins

Yield: 12 servings

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2 tablespoons cold water
  • Apricot jam for filling
  • 1 egg white
  • 1/4 cup coconut

Steps:

  1. Mix flour, baking powder, salt, and butter to form crumbs.
  2. Add sugar, egg yolk, and cold water to form dough.
  3. Roll and cut into tartlet shapes.
  4. Fill each with apricot jam.
  5. Whip egg white with coconut and spoon on top.
  6. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 20 minutes.
  7. Cool and enjoy!
