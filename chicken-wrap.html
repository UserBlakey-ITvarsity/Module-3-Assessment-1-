Chicken Wrap

Prep Time: 15 mins

Yield: 4 servings

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless chicken breasts, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 4 large tortillas
  • 1/2 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon hot sauce (optional)

Steps:

  1. Season the sliced chicken with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and paprika.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and cook the chicken until golden brown and fully cooked.
  3. Warm the tortillas in a dry pan or microwave.
  4. Spread mayonnaise and hot sauce on each tortilla.
  5. Layer lettuce, tomatoes, and cooked chicken on top.
  6. Fold the sides of the tortilla and roll it tightly.
  7. Slice in half and serve warm.
