Homemade Burger

Prep Time: 15 mins

Yield: 4 servings

Ingredients:

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 4 burger buns
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • Lettuce, tomato, and onion (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon ketchup

Steps:

  1. Mix ground beef with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  2. Shape into 4 equal-sized patties.
  3. Heat a pan or grill over medium-high heat and cook patties for about 4 minutes per side.
  4. Place a slice of cheddar cheese on each patty and let it melt.
  5. Toast burger buns lightly.
  6. Spread mayonnaise and ketchup on the buns.
  7. Assemble the burger with lettuce, tomato, onion, and the cooked patty.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
