Strawberry Milkshake

Prep Time: 5 mins

Yield: 2 servings

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fresh strawberries
  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
  • Whipped cream (optional)

Steps:

  1. Blend strawberries, ice cream, and milk until smooth.
  2. Sweeten with honey if desired.
  3. Pour into a tall glass.
  4. Top with whipped cream for extra deliciousness.
  5. Enjoy your creamy strawberry milkshake!
