Chocolate Mousse

Prep Time: 10 mins

Yield: 4 servings

Ingredients:

  • 200g dark chocolate
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 eggs, separated
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • Pinch of salt

Steps:

  1. Melt chocolate with butter.
  2. Mix in egg yolks and set aside.
  3. Whip egg whites and sugar until fluffy.
  4. Fold into chocolate mixture.
  5. Whip cream and gently mix in.
  6. Chill for 2 hours and enjoy!
