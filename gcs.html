Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Prep Time: 10 mins

Yield: 4 servings

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup butter, softened
  • 1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese
  • 8 Italian bread slices
  • 4 (3/4-oz.) provolone cheese slices
  • 4 (3/4-oz.) mozzarella cheese slices

Steps:

  1. Stir together butter and Parmesan cheese in a small bowl.
  2. Spread 1 1/2 tsp. butter mixture on one side of each bread slice.
  3. Place 4 bread slices, buttered sides down, on wax paper.
  4. Top with provolone and mozzarella cheeses, then add remaining bread slices, buttered sides up.
  5. Cook sandwiches on a hot griddle or in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  6. Gently press with a spatula, cooking for about 4 minutes on each side, until golden brown and cheese is melted.
