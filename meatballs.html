Baked Meatballs

Prep Time: 25 mins

Yield: 4 servings

Ingredients:

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley
  • 1/2 cup marinara sauce

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, mix ground beef, breadcrumbs, egg, salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, Parmesan cheese, and parsley.
  3. Shape the mixture into small meatballs and place them on the baking sheet.
  4. Bake for 15-20 minutes until golden brown and cooked through.
  5. Heat marinara sauce in a pan and toss the baked meatballs in the sauce.
  6. Serve warm with pasta or bread.
