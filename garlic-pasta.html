Garlic Butter Pasta

Prep Time: 20 mins

Yield: 4 servings

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces dried pasta
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped

Steps:

  1. Boil water in a medium-sized pot, add salt, and cook pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining.
  3. In a skillet, melt 2 tablespoons of butter over medium heat and lightly saut√© the minced garlic.
  4. Add the hot pasta along with some reserved pasta water, tossing until a thick sauce forms.
  5. Mix in the remaining butter, Parmesan cheese, and chopped parsley.
  6. Season with salt and black pepper, toss well, and serve warm.
