Milk Tart

Prep Time: 20 mins

Yield: 8 servings

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 2 tablespoons cornflour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Cinnamon for dusting

Steps:

  1. Heat milk until warm.
  2. Mix flour, cornflour, eggs, sugar, and vanilla.
  3. Slowly add to milk and stir.
  4. Pour into tart shell and chill.
  5. Sprinkle cinnamon and serve!
